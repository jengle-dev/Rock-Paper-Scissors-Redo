Online Rock, Paper, Scissors Game

Play against the computer in a game of
Rock, Paper, Scissors

This game is between you, the user, and "me", the computer.

The Rules:

  • Rock wins against scissors because rocks can smash scissors.
  • Paper wins against rock because paper can cover a rock.
  • Scissors win against paper because scissors cut paper.
  • If both players throw the same choice, it is considered a tie, and play resumes until there is a clear winner.
